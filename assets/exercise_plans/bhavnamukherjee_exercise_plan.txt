**Day 1: Saturday, July 27th, 2024**

- **Evening (30 minutes):**
    - **Cool-down (10 minutes):** Static stretches like holding a hamstring stretch or a quad stretch for 30 seconds each. 
    - **Rest and Relaxation:** Engage in a calming activity like meditation or deep breathing exercises.

- **Hydration:** Bhavna should drink plenty of water throughout the day, especially before, during, and after exercise.
- **Listen to Your Body:** This plan is a guideline. Bhavna should listen to her body and adjust the intensity and duration of exercises as needed.

- **Seek Professional Advice:** Bhavna can consult with a doctor, physical therapist, or certified fitness professional for personalized recommendations and guidance.
